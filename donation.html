How Your Donations Are Used

40% of donations go towards delivering food and essential supplies to Palestinian families in need.

30% of your contributions fund critical medical aid, including emergency treatment and long-term care.

20% supports the education of children who have been affected by the conflict, ensuring access to learning resources.

10% goes towards rebuilding homes, schools, and community centers that have been destroyed during the conflict.

The Impact of Your Contribution

Every dollar you donate directly contributes to changing lives. By supporting the Palestine Peace Initiative, you help provide food to families, medical care to the injured, and education to children. You also aid in rebuilding the infrastructure destroyed by the ongoing conflict.

Your generosity allows us to respond swiftly to crises and continue our long-term efforts to support the people of Palestine. Together, we can make a lasting impact in a region where help is desperately needed.
